FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Yes. DateWise is free to download and use with up to 50 reminders and all core features. Pro is an optional one-time purchase for cloud backup, custom types, and unlimited reminders — no subscription ever.
No account is ever required. DateWise works fully offline. Your data stays on your device. Optional Google Drive backup (Pro) does require a Google account, but that's entirely your choice — the app works perfectly without it.
DateWise reads birthday dates stored in your phone contacts and creates reminders automatically. It only reads — never writes or modifies your contacts. You'll see a standard Android permission dialog first. The import can be re-run anytime from Settings.
Yes, completely. All notifications are scheduled locally on your device using Android's alarm system. They fire even if the app is closed, the phone is in airplane mode, or you have no data connection.
DateWise Pro is a one-time in-app purchase (₹299 in India, pricing varies by region). It unlocks Google Drive backup, custom reminder types, unlimited reminders, and the Pro stats dashboard. There are no subscriptions — pay once, keep it forever.
Your data never leaves your device unless you enable Google Drive backup. We collect no analytics, run no ads, and share nothing with third parties. The app also supports biometric locking (fingerprint / face unlock) for an added layer of security.
With Pro, you can create your own reminder types beyond the 9 built-in ones. Give it a name, pick an emoji from over 120 options, and it appears everywhere in the app — filter bar, add screen, and notifications.
