Features

Built for privacy-conscious people

PixWise doesn't upload your photos, scan your face, or phone home. It's a gallery app that does its job and stays out of your way.

🔐

Secure Space

Move private photos to an encrypted hidden vault. Protected by biometric or PIN authentication. Invisible in guest mode.

👤

Guest Mode

Hand your phone to anyone without worry. Guest mode hides Secure Space, restricts albums, and disables sensitive features.

🖼️

Full-Featured Viewer

Pinch-to-zoom, double-tap zoom, swipe between photos, video playback, EXIF info, rotation, slideshow, and wallpaper setting.

📁

Smart Albums

Auto-organized by folder. Pin your favorites to the top. Hide albums you don't want to see. Copy, move, and rename with ease.

🗑️

Recycle Bin

Deleted a photo by accident? Recycle Bin keeps it safe for 30 days. Restore with a single tap—no cloud needed.

🔍

Instant Search

Find any photo by name, type, date, or size. Debounced search keeps things snappy even with thousands of photos.

🛡️

Privacy by Design

No internet permission. No analytics SDK. No account required. Your photos never leave your device. PixWise is built for people who believe privacy isn't optional—it's a right.

Why PixWise?

Your stock gallery can't do this

Most gallery apps are either bloated with AI features you didn't ask for, or they quietly upload your photos to someone else's server. PixWise is different.

🚫

No Cloud Uploads

Your photos stay on your device. Period. No sync, no backup we control, no surprise bandwidth usage.

⚡

Lightning Fast

Built with Jetpack Compose and optimized thumbnail caching. Scrolls smoothly through 50,000+ photos.

🎨

Material You Theming

Follows your system theme automatically. Light, dark, and dynamic color support. Looks native on every Android device.

📱

Configurable Grid

2, 3, or 4 column layouts. Choose timeline or album view as your default. Your gallery, your way.
