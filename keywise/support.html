Frequently asked questions

Can I use KeyWise on multiple devices?

KeyWise does not provide cloud sync in current production builds. You can move data manually by creating an encrypted local backup file and importing it on another device.

Does KeyWise work offline?

Yes. Vault operations are local-first and available offline. Some optional checks, such as update checks or breach checks, may use network access when enabled.

Is Premium a subscription?

No. Premium is a one-time purchase that increases vault and credential limits. It is not a monthly or yearly subscription.

What if I forget my lock credential?

Use your recovery path if configured. If both lock credential and recovery options are unavailable, vault recovery may not be possible by design.

How do refunds work?

In-app purchase refunds follow Google Play policies and your Google account purchase terms.

Security note: never share full credential values by email. If needed, share only redacted screenshots and steps.

Current product scope reminder

KeyWise current production builds are local-first. They do not include cloud sync, Google Drive sync, family sharing, or member invite features.
