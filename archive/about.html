The Founder

Vision-led. Purpose-driven.
Built to last.

WiselyRise Solutions is founded and led by Menagapriya, who brings a clear vision to every product in the Wise portfolio. Under her direction, the studio builds apps and software that are genuinely useful, genuinely respectful of privacy, and genuinely free of the dark patterns that plague so much of the industry.

The "Wise" in every app name is a deliberate promise: think carefully before you build. Every product in the family must earn its place — through real-world utility, thoughtful design, and honest value. If it doesn't meet that bar, it doesn't ship.

"We build software we're proud of — tools that don't exploit the people who use them, that earn trust through quality and integrity, and that respect your time, your data, and your intelligence."

— Menagapriya, Founder & Director · WiselyRise Solutions

WiselyRise's scope extends beyond its own app portfolio. The studio also takes on select client projects — delivering custom software, design, and prototyping with the same exacting standards applied to every Wise app. The same philosophy applies regardless of who we build for.

Roots & Reach

Built in the heart of
Tamil Nadu.

WiselyRise Solutions is headquartered in Sivagiri, Erode district, Tamil Nadu — a region known for its textiles, commerce, and entrepreneurial drive. Being rooted here directly shapes the apps we build and the problems we choose to solve.

Tamil is our native language, which means every Tamil localisation in our apps is a first-class experience — not an afterthought bolted on for the Play Store listing. We understand the real-world contexts of Indian households: tracking jewellery wealth, managing family reminders, securing credentials, and protecting personal photos.

But the ambition isn't local. Our apps ship with support for 28+ locales — including German, Portuguese (Brazil), Indonesian, Spanish (LatAm), Swahili, Urdu, and Russian — because thoughtful tools deserve a global audience.
