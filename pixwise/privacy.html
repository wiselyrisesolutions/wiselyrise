Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary

PixWise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your photos never leave your device. There are no analytics, no tracking pixels, no ad networks, and no cloud services.

Data We Collect

None. PixWise operates entirely on-device. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Location data
  • Photo metadata or file contents
  • Device identifiers

Permissions

PixWise requests the following Android permissions solely to function as a gallery app:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO — To display your photos and videos from device storage.
  • USE_BIOMETRIC — To authenticate access to Secure Space using your fingerprint or face.
  • SET_WALLPAPER — To allow you to set a photo as your device wallpaper from the viewer.

No data accessed through these permissions is ever transmitted off your device.

Secure Space

Photos moved to Secure Space are stored in app-private internal storage, inaccessible to other apps. They are protected by biometric authentication or PIN. Secure Space data is never backed up to cloud services and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

PixWise does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or cloud services. The app has no internet permission and cannot communicate with any server.

Children's Privacy

PixWise does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since we collect no data at all, there is no personal information at risk.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at contact@wiselyrise.in.
