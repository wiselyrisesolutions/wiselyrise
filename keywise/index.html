Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use KeyWise without internet?

Yes. KeyWise is designed as an offline-first app, so your local vault access does not depend on internet connectivity.

What happens if I forget my lock?

Use your configured recovery mechanism. Keep your recovery details safe because the app is designed to avoid server-side recovery backdoors.

Can I export my data?

You can export an encrypted backup format and CSV export (when vaults are unlocked) for migration and ownership control.
