How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No account. No setup wizard. Just download and start logging your gold.

01

Download & Open

Install GramWise from the Play Store. No signup, no email, no permissions required beyond biometric lock. Just open and go.

02

Add Your Items

Tap the + button and use the step-by-step wizard to log your first piece. Select metal, form, weight, purity, purchase details, and assign to a family member.

03

Watch It Update

Live metal prices auto-update your entire portfolio value. Check what your gold is worth today, track your savings goals, and always know where each piece is stored.
