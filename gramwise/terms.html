2. Description of Service

GramWise is a personal gold and jewellery portfolio tracker for Android devices. It allows users to:

  • Log precious metal items (gold, silver, platinum, palladium) with details such as weight, purity, and purchase price.
  • Assign items to family members and storage locations.
  • View live precious metal market prices fetched from a third-party API.
  • Set and track savings goals related to gold purchases.
  • Secure their portfolio data with biometric or PIN-based app lock.

The App operates primarily in offline mode — all user data is stored locally on the device. Internet connectivity is only used to fetch live metal prices.
