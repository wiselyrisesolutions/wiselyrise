Account & Login

How do I reset my master password?

Your master password is the only key to decrypt your vault. If you forget it, use your Recovery Code (saved during setup) to regain access. If you've lost both, your vault cannot be recovered—this is a security feature that ensures nobody but you can access your data.

Can I use KeyWise on multiple devices?

Yes! Your Premium tier includes cloud backup and sync. Export your vault from one device and import it on another using the encrypted .keywise backup file. Your data stays encrypted throughout the process.

Security & Encryption

Is my data really encrypted?

Yes, 100%. All credentials are encrypted on your device using XChaCha20-Poly1305 (military-grade authenticated encryption). Your master password is derived using Argon2id. We use zero-knowledge architecture—your encryption keys never leave your device, so even WiselyRise cannot decrypt your data.

How does password health checking work?

We check your passwords against a database of known breaches using k-anonymity (a privacy technique). Your actual password is never sent to our servers—only a partial hash is sent, and we return only breach matches for your review.

Features & Usage

Can I organize my credentials into categories?

Yes! KeyWise supports rich organization. Create vaults for different aspects of your life (personal, work, family), and use categories within each vault to organize credentials by type (logins, financial, documents, etc.).

Does KeyWise work offline?

Yes. All your credentials are stored locally on your device. You can access them anytime—online or offline. The only features requiring internet are cloud backup and password breach checks.

Premium & Billing

What does Premium include?

Premium is a one-time ₹99 (or $1.99 USD) lifetime unlock. It includes unlimited vaults, unlimited credentials, and all core features. Premium is a lifetime purchase—no subscriptions, no renewals.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds for Google Play purchases follow Google's standard refund policy (usually 48 hours from purchase). Contact us if you have questions—we're happy to help!
