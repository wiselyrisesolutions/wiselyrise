Security & Privacy First

DocuWise prioritizes your privacy and security above all else.

🔐 End-to-End Security Your documents live in your personal Google Drive. We never store, process, or access your documents on our servers.
✅ Zero Backend Architecture No servers, no databases, no backend to compromise. Minimal attack surface, maximum privacy.
🔑 Google Sign-In Secure authentication using official Google Sign-In. Your credentials never touch our systems.
📲 Offline First Works completely offline. Sync happens securely in the background when online.
🛡️ Biometric Lock Optional fingerprint/face unlock adds an extra security layer on top of your PIN.
🔄 Automatic Updates Regular security updates keep your app protected against emerging threats.
