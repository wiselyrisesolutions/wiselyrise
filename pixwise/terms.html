2. Description of Service

PixWise is a local gallery application for Android that lets you browse, organize, and manage photos and videos stored on your device. Key features include:

  • Timeline & Albums view — Chronological timeline and album-based organization.
  • Lite Mode — One-tap hiding of private albums when handing your device to others.
  • Secure Space — Biometric-protected private vault stored in app-private storage.
  • Recycle Bin — 30-day soft-delete recovery for deleted media.
  • Rename, rotate, wallpaper, share — In-viewer media management tools.
  • Smart search — Search by date, type, and file size.
