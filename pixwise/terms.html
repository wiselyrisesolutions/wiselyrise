Secure Space

The Secure Space feature moves files to app-private storage protected by biometric or PIN authentication. Please note:

  • Files in Secure Space are deleted when you uninstall the App.
  • If you forget your PIN and biometric authentication fails, there is no recovery mechanism for Secure Space files.
  • We recommend keeping backups of important files outside of Secure Space.

Deleted items are moved to a Recycle Bin and automatically purged after 30 days. Items in the Recycle Bin can be restored before the purge period expires.
