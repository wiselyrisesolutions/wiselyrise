The Founder

Vision-led.
Purpose-driven.

WiselyRise Solutions is founded and led by Menagapriya, who brings a clear vision to every product in the Wise portfolio. Under her direction, the studio builds apps and software that are genuinely useful, genuinely respectful of privacy, and genuinely free of the dark patterns that plague so much of the industry.

The 'Wise' in every app name is a deliberate promise: think carefully before you build. Every product in the family must earn its place - through real-world utility, thoughtful design, and honest value. If it does not meet that bar, it does not ship.

"We build software we are proud of - tools that do not exploit the people who use them, that earn trust through quality and integrity, and that respect your time, your data, and your intelligence."

- Menagapriya, Founder & Director

WiselyRise's scope extends beyond its own app portfolio. The studio also takes on select client projects - delivering custom software, design, and prototyping with the same exacting standards applied to every Wise app.
