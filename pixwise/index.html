👁 Lite Mode

One tap.
Your phone,
your rules.

Before handing your phone to kids, guests, or anyone else — activate Lite Mode in a single tap. Private albums disappear instantly. One tap to bring them right back when you're done.

  • ✓Per-album visibility — you decide exactly what shows
  • ✓Biometric-protected deactivation, no accidental toggle
  • ✓Always-on Lite Mode option in settings
  • ✓Instant — no extra menus, no delay
🔒 Secure Space

A private vault
inside your gallery.

Secure Space is PixWise's dedicated vault — a biometric-locked folder completely invisible to other apps. Move photos in with a tap. Export as encrypted ZIP anytime.

  • ✓Biometric or device-credential authentication
  • ✓Other apps can never read or list Secure Space
  • ✓Completely hidden in Lite Mode — doubly protected
  • ✓Export as encrypted ZIP directly to Downloads
